Xi Jinping: Adhere to "One Country, Two Systems" and Promote the Unification of the Motherland


——The report of the 19th National Congress

Comrade Xi Jinping stressed in his report to the Nineteenth National Congress that "one country, two systems" should be upheld to promote the reunification of the motherland.

XXi Jinping pointed out: facts have proved that "one country, two systems" is the best solution to the problems left over from history in Hong Kong and Macao, and is also the best system for maintaining long-term prosperity and stability after the return of Hong Kong and Macao to China.

Xi Jinping stressed: to maintain the long-term prosperity and stability of Hong Kong and Macao, we must fully and accurately implement the principles of "one country, two systems", "Hong Kong people ruling Hong Kong", "Macao people ruling Macao" and a high degree of autonomy, and act strictly in accordance with the Constitution and the basic Law. Improve the system and mechanism related to the implementation of the basic Law. We should support the government of the Special Administrative region and the Chief Executive in administering the administration according to law, actively act, unite and lead Hong Kong and Macao people from all walks of life to work together for development, promote harmony, safeguard and improve people's livelihood, advance democracy in an orderly manner, and safeguard social stability. Fulfilling the constitutional responsibility of safeguarding national sovereignty, security and development interests

It is necessary to support the integration of Hong Kong and Macao into the overall situation of national development, focus on the construction of Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macau Greater Bay Area, cooperation between Guangdong, Hong Kong and Macao, and regional cooperation in the Pan-Pearl River Delta, comprehensively promote mutually beneficial cooperation between the mainland and Hong Kong and Macao, and formulate and improve and facilitate Hong Kong. Policy measures for the Development of Macao residents in the mainland

Xi Jinping said: We adhere to the Patriots as the mainstay of "Hong Kong people ruling Hong Kong" and "Macao people ruling Macao", develop and strengthen patriotism, love Hong Kong and love Macao, strengthen the national consciousness and patriotism of Hong Kong and Macao compatriots, and let Hong Kong and Macao compatriots share the motherland. The people share the historical responsibility of national rejuvenation and share the great glory of the prosperity and prosperity of the motherland.

On the Taiwan issue, Xi Jinping said that resolving the Taiwan issue and realizing the complete reunification of the motherland are the common aspirations of all the Chinese people and the fundamental interests of the Chinese nation. We must continue to adhere to the principle of "peaceful reunification, one country, two systems", promote the peaceful development of cross-strait relations, and advance the process of peaceful reunification of the motherland.

He emphasized that the "1992 Consensus" embodying the one-China principle clearly defines the fundamental nature of cross-strait relations and is the key to ensuring the peaceful development of cross-strait relations. Recognizing the historical facts of the "1992 Consensus" and agreeing that the two sides of the strait belong to the same China, the two sides can conduct dialogues and resolve the issues of concern to the compatriots on both sides of the strait. There will be no obstacles to any party or group in Taiwan to interact with the mainland.

Xi Jinping said: We uphold the "cross-strait family" concept, respect Taiwan's existing social system and Taiwanese compatriots' lifestyle, and are willing to take the lead in sharing opportunities for mainland development with Taiwan compatriots. It will expand cross-strait economic and cultural exchanges and cooperation, achieve mutual benefit and reciprocity, and gradually provide Taiwan compatriots with the same treatment as mainland compatriots in studying, starting, working, and living in the mainland, and enhancing the well-being of Taiwan compatriots. It will promote compatriots on both sides of the strait to jointly promote Chinese culture and promote spiritual harmony.

Xi Jinping emphasized that resolutely safeguarding national sovereignty and territorial integrity will never tolerate a repeat of the historical tragedy of the country's division. All activities that split the motherland will be resolutely opposed by all Chinese. We have a firm will, full confidence, and sufficient ability to defeat any form of "Taiwan independence" separatist plot. No one, any organization, any political party, or any form of Chinese territory will be allowed to be separated from China at any time and in any form.

Xi Jinping said that realizing the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation is the common dream of all Chinese people. We firmly believe that as long as all Chinese people, including Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan compatriots, conform to the historical trend, share the national ideology, and firmly hold the national destiny in their own hands, they will certainly be able to create a beautiful future for the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation.
