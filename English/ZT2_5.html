WEAVING THE SAFETY NET FOR PEPLE'S LIVELIHOOD AND WELFARE -- A SUMMARY OF THE ACHIEVEMENTS OF SOCIAL DEVELOPMENT SINCE THE EIGHTEEN PARTY'S GREAT ACHIEVEMENTS

03 October 2017, 16:17    source：Xinhua News Agency

Original title: weaving the safety net for people's livelihood and welfare -- a summary of the achievements of social development since the eighteen Party's great achievements

Since the eighteen Party's great achievements, China has made great achievements in the development of people's livelihood. The central government has introduced a series of measures to weave a tight safety net for the people's livelihood.

The policy of Huimin has been increasing.

Ban Yan village in Huzhu Tu Autonomous County of Qinghai province is a poor village. Traffic is inconvenient and scattered in residence. After the relocation of immigrants, * every villager lived on a clean and spacious quadrangle courtyard through the development of local characteristic eight eyebrow pig, and this year it will get rid of poverty as a whole. The villagers talked about the changes and their faces were full of joy.

With the precise Poverty Alleviation Policies and measures taking root, from 2013 to 2016, China's rural poverty-stricken population has lifted 55.64 million people out of poverty. The target of eliminating poverty in 2017 was more than 10 million.

The public will summon the mission and show the feelings. In the past five years, a series of people's livelihood initiatives have brought more sense of gain to the people, and created a new realm of benign interaction between economic development and people's livelihood improvement: the pension continues to rise continuously on the basis of the "eight consecutive increases"; the rural minimum living standard doubles in five years, and the growth rate is far exceeding the GDP growth rate. From 2,067.8 yuan per capita in 2012 to 3,611 yuan per capita in 2016, the construction of affordable housing projects has been steadily promoted, and the policy of education and medical care for the people has been steadily strengthened. China has woven the world's largest universal basic medical insurance network, and has established a serious illness insurance system for urban and rural residents. At present, the participation rate of urban and rural residents is stable at more than 95%, and the per capita financial subsidy standard of basic medical insurance for urban and rural residents has increased from 240 yuan in 2012 to 450 yuan in 2017.

Traffic "acceleration" changes life.

On September 21, Fuxing China Standard EMU operated at 350 kilometers per hour on the Beijing-Shanghai high-speed railway, marking that China has become the world's fastest commercial operation of high-speed rail.

China has built the world's largest, fastest-running high-speed railway network with fully independent intellectual property rights. The long-distance high-speed railway galloping on the land of Shenzhou is changing people's lives with unprecedented "acceleration" and providing new impetus for economic development.

Over the past 5 years, China's comprehensive transport infrastructure network has been improving. More comprehensive passenger transport hubs have been built, and more and more high-speed railway stations have been connected to subway, long-distance bus, bus and other hubs in all directions. Integrated transportation has better integration and convenience, and people's travel experience is better. Mobile App booking, electronic payment, self help ticket collection is very common, traffic travel service is more convenient and fast.

By the end of 2016, the operating mileage of the country's railways had reached 124,000 kilometers, of which the high-speed railway had exceeded 22,000 kilometers. The total mileage of the highway reaches 4 million 960 thousand km, of which the mileage of expressways exceeds 130 thousand km. There are 2 221 berths in the harbor with a total mileage of 127,000 km in inland waterways, including 12,100 km in high-grade waterways. The airport has been awarded 218 airports, including 28 hub airports with more than ten million passengers.

New impetus for new formats.

Employment is the mother of people's livelihood.

"A cell phone can realize the dream of starting an undertaking." At the end of August, Zhang Rufeng, the head of Henan Luoyang Base Silver Electronic Technology Co., Ltd., stayed at home and applied on the Internet through his mobile phone. He got the first business license of Henan Province to be handled electronically. At present, the company has entered the pre-operation state.

For the benefit of the people, silk hair will flourishing. The Eighteenth National Congress of the CPC has started the process of deepening the reform in an all-round way, and has introduced a series of measures to reform the commercial system. It has made great efforts to create an environment conducive to the development of the private and individual economy, a market environment of fair competition, a high-quality and efficient service environment, so as to enable the private and individual economy to enter a track of sustained, rapid and healthy development. To play an important role in China's economic development.

From the early online store to today's various forms of "Internet +", a variety of new forms of business emerged constantly, the overwhelming number of small and micro enterprises, opened up the capillary of China's economic development, to add impetus for economic development.

By the first half of 2017, China's private enterprises had reached 24.9708 million households, an increase of 20.94% over the same period of last year, registered capital of 155.1 trillion yuan, an increase of 40.92% over the same period of last year, and individual industrial and commercial households of 61.5397 million households, an increase of 10.45% over the same period of last year, and the amount of capital reached 4874.439 billion yuan, an increase of 21.30%. With the development of private economy, private enterprises and individual business households have become the main channels to absorb employment. On average, each private enterprise can bring 8 people to work, and each individual business household can bring 2.8 people to work.

Since the 18th National Congress of the Communist Party of China, a series of measures have been introduced in succession to promote entrepreneurship innovation, new momentum growth and expand employment. Statistics show that more than 10 million new jobs have been created in China for five consecutive years, and people's pockets are getting bigger and bigger. (Xinhua news agency, Beijing, October 3) reporter Zhao Wenjun, Qi Zhongxi)
