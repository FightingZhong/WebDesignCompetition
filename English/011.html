Ke xiaohai: the "ruling family" who serve the people and never forget the original intention


　　"A rich man is not rich, a rich village is rich". This is yan 'an huangling county solowan village party branch secretary ke xiaohai in more than 10 years as the work of the village secretary. He led the development of the whole village industry by grasping the party building, and led the villagers to work hard, so that the villagers of solow bay village embarked on the road of becoming rich and well-off.
　　The village of soloway, located deep in the mountains, is now well-known as a rich village, a model village and a civilized village. Speaking of the good days, the villagers all say that it is thanks to ke xiaohai.
Lu jianmin, a villager from solowan village in huangling county, said, "I am upright, crisp and fair. Everything is done for the collective, without selfishness."
　　More than a decade ago, the village of soloway was a typical poor village, with people living on a shoestring budget. In 2000, ke xiaohai, only 24 years old, was elected village party secretary. Since taking office, he has led party members and villagers to build roads, improve water, build Bridges and build new villages for migrant workers. In 2012, solowan village seized the opportunity of more and more tourists from nearby huangling national forest park, and vigorously developed rural tourism, opening a new way for villagers to increase income.
　　Ke xiaohai, party branch secretary of solowan village, huangling county, said: "after we have built this, that is to say, the following industries will probably be endless, and I feel that I have found this way for my masses, and found the right way."
　　In order to make the whole village turn into a rope and unite to start a business, ke xiaohai created the "party member value week system" in the village. Every party member in the village took turns to be a village official for one week. In this way, all the party members of the village became village cadres, and all the members actively participated in the construction and development of the village.
　　Ke xiaohai, party secretary of solowan village, huangling county, said, "as a party secretary, I will think how to make our masses richer. This is what you should do as a party secretary."
　　Over the past 17 years, the collective economy of solow bay village has increased from zero to more than 18 million yuan. In 2016, the per capita income of solowan village was more than 20,000 yuan, and each villager still had a collective dividend of 5,000 yuan by the end of the year. Ke xiaohai has been awarded national model worker, national outstanding communist party member and more than 20 honorary titles.
　　Zhang junfeng, director of the organization department of huangling county committee, said, "the success of solowan village is closely related to ke xiaohai's loyalty to the party, being strict with himself, being close to the masses and daring to work.
