New era and New Journey leading to consensus


—— Focus interview

The 19th National Congress of the Communist Party of China ended victoriously in Beijing today. The Congress has made a series of important achievements, which is of great significance to the decisive victory in building a well-off society in an all-round way and the great victory of socialism with Chinese characteristics in the new era. The Congress adopted a resolution on the Constitution of the Communist Party of China (Amendment) and made more than 100 amendments to the current Party Constitution. The most important amendments focus on four aspects: first, the incorporation of Xi Jinping's ideology of socialism with Chinese characteristics in the new era into the Party Constitution; and second, the inclusion of General Secretary Xi Jinping in the Party Central Committee and the central position of the whole party in the party constitution. Third, we should write into the Party Constitution General Secretary Xi Jinping's new ideas, and new strategies for governing the country. Fourth, he should write into the party's constitution the theoretical achievements of administering the party comprehensively and strictly since the 18th National Congress of the Party and the achievements of the system. Today, we invite 19 delegates and authoritative experts to interpret

At today's closing meeting, the resolution on the Constitution of the Communist Party of China (Amendment) was adopted, and the existing party constitution was amended. In the view of the deputies, the contents of these amendments are very timely and necessary.

Ma Xuming, a deputy to the 19th National Congress of the CPC, said: "this revision of the Party Constitution is in accordance with the needs of the party's theoretical innovation and practical development, and the Party Constitution has been revised in a timely manner, complying with the requirements of the new era, and realizing once again the advance with the times of the party constitution."
