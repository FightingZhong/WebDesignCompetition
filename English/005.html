People's Daily commentator: firmly grasp the historic mission of the CPC in the new era

——Study and implement the 19th party spirit


　　To realize the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation is the greatest dream of the Chinese nation since modern times. To realize this great dream is the historical mission of the CPC since its founding. 　　"The great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation is by no means possible with ease and with the beating of a gong and a drum." The 19th CPC national congress gives an affectionate review of the arduous efforts and great achievements made by our party in the past 96 years to realize its historical mission, elaborates in depth the historical mission of the CPC in the new era, and gives ideological mobilization and direction for all party members to better shoulder their historical mission in the new era. An important part of learning to implement the 19th CPC spirit is to firmly grasp the historical mission of the CPC in the new era, and to unite the hundreds of millions of people in the great practice of socialism with Chinese characteristics in the new era to build the great power of the Chinese dream.
　　Mission directions. To firmly grasp the historical mission of the CPC in the new era, we should first of all deeply understand the historical logic and realistic logic of China's road in the past 96 years. In the historical process of revolution, construction and reform, our party has united with the people through, pay a huge sacrifice, come at the expense of the blood and life of deep understanding, to achieve the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation, must overthrow the pressure on the Chinese people's head of imperialism, feudalism and bureaucratic capitalism three mountain, realize the national independence and liberation, national unity, social stability; It is necessary to establish advanced social system in line with China's reality. The party and the people must have the courage to reform and open up in keeping with the trend of The Times and the will of the people, so that the cause of the party and the people will always be full of strong impetus to forge ahead. It is these regular understandings that have enabled our party to overcome seemingly insurmountable difficulties relying on the people, create one miraculous miracle after another, and make great historical contributions to the Chinese nation.
　　Mission leads the future. To firmly grasp the historical mission of the CPC in the new era, we must have a profound understanding of the internal connection between the realization of great dreams in the new era and the great struggle, construction of great projects and promotion of great causes. The 19th CPC national congress has elaborated in depth the rich connotation of the great struggle, the great project and the great cause to realize the great dream. To study and implement the 19th party spirit, we should fully understand the close relationship between the four greats. Only by carrying out the great struggle with many new historical characteristics and achieving the "five more self-consciousness" emphasized by the 19th CPC national congress can we remove all difficulties and obstacles for the realization of the great dream. Only by deepening the great new project of party building and constantly enhancing the party's political leadership, ideological leadership, mass organization and social appeal can we provide strong political guarantee for the realization of the great dream. Only by consistently adhering to and developing socialism with Chinese characteristics and more consciously enhancing the "four self-confidence" can we pave the way for the realization of the great dream. Among the "four greats", what plays a decisive role is the great new project of party building. Combined with great struggle, a great career, and push the great project, the practice of the great dream it is to uphold and improve the party's leadership, to be more strong and unwavering commitment to the party construction, to ensure that our party has always been one of the leading enterprises in times, has become the mainstay of the national people's, always be strong leadership core, the communist party of China we will be able to shoulder the historical mission of the new era, new great historical contribution for the Chinese nation.
　　Starting from shikumen, Shanghai, in the course of the spectacular history of 96 years, our party, whether weak or strong, has never changed its original intention, regardless of favorable or adverse circumstances. Today, we are closer than at any time in history to the goal of the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation. We are fully confident and capable of realizing the great dream and delivering new and better answers to history and the people.
