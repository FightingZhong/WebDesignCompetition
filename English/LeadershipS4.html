Hu Chunhua, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee, is in the forefront of the new journey to speed up the construction of socialist modernization


——The report of the 19th National Congress

General Secretary Xi Jinping's report to the Nineteenth National Congress of the CPC is a political declaration and a guide to action for our party to enter a new era, start a new journey, and renew a new chapter. The report puts forward the objective task of building a well-off society in an all-around way in the new era, starting a new journey to build a socialist modern country in an all-round way, and putting the realization of socialist modernization on the agenda of the Party and the state. Of great and far-reaching historical significance

　 Realizing modernization is the unremitting pursuit of countless people with lofty ideals since modern times. Since the founding of the Party, our party has taken the realization of modernization and the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation as its historical mission and goal of struggle. After years of continuous efforts, we have solved the problem of food and clothing, the overall realization of a well-off society will soon be built. Now that we have entered a new era, the realization of modernization has become a major task before us. The report proposes to use 30 years to build a socialist modern country in an all-round way and to carry it forward in "two fifteen years", which points out the way forward for the modernization drive and provides a clear timetable and road map.

In April this year, General Secretary Xi Jinping gave important instructions on Guangdong's work, explicitly demanding that Guangdong be in the forefront of building a well-off society in an all-round way and speeding up the new journey of building socialist modernization. Guangdong's modernization construction has a good foundation and conditions, and the innovative economy is being accelerated to form, and we must strive to be in the forefront of the new journey of modernization construction. Guangdong will conscientiously implement the spirit of the Nineteenth National Congress of the Party, take Xi Jinping's new era of socialism with Chinese characteristics as its guide, and write a brilliant new chapter in the development of Guangdong in the new era.
