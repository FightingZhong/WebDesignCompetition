new era, new journey, win, comprehensive well-off battle


——Focus interview

At present, China's development is still in an important period of strategic opportunities, with bright prospects and severe challenges, according to the report of the Nineteenth National Congress. The report stressed: in particular, we must resolutely fight against major risks, eliminate major risks, accurately combat poverty, and prevent pollution, so that building a well-off society in an all-round way is recognized by the people and can stand the test of history. In order to fulfill the historical task of socialism with Chinese characteristics in the new era, the Nineteenth National Congress report has made a series of important strategic arrangements. Today, we will continue to invite 19 NPC deputies and authoritative experts to interpret for you.

The report of the Nineteenth National Congress put forward that from now to 2020, it is a decisive period to build a well-off society in an all-round way. In particular, we must resolutely fight the battle of preventing and defusing major risks, accurately eradicating poverty, and preventing and controlling pollution. So, how to fight these three major battles? first, let's focus on preventing and defusing major risks.

　 "to prevent and defuse major risks, I understand that the most important part of this is financial risk," said Yang Weimin, a member of the drafting group of the Nineteenth National Congress document. "in the past five years, the General Secretary has made previous decisions on economic work. Both regard risk prevention as an important content and always remind local governments at all levels and relevant departments to keep the bottom line. What is the bottom line? That is, there is no systemic financial risk. Of course, there is no systemic financial risk in our country now, but the small risk is constant. "
