People's Daily commentator: unwaveringly to make the party stronger and stronger

——Study and implement the 19th CPC spirit


　　Socialism with Chinese characteristics has entered a new era. In the new era, we need to take new steps. The key is that our party should comprehensively enhance political leadership, ideological leadership, mass organization and social appeal, and maintain vigorous vitality and combat effectiveness.
　　"If the party is to unite and lead the people in carrying out the great struggle, advancing the great cause and realizing the great dream, it must unswervingly uphold and improve the party's leadership and make the party stronger and stronger." The grasps seeks happiness for the people of China, the party's 19 for the beginner's mind seeks the revival of Chinese nation and the mission, focus on the leading enterprises in the era of "always, people sincerely support, to self revolution, stand the test all kinds of waves, vibrant marxist ruling party" for the construction of the target, put forward the new era, the party's construction general requirements, the advancing the new great project of strategic deployment, is sharpens party's ruling ability and leading level of the top design, is to promote the further development of comprehensive governing party's basic follow.
　　Over the past 96 years, our party has been able to unite and lead the people to overcome one seemingly impossible difficulty after another, and create one extraordinary miracle after another. Since the 18th national congress of the communist party of China in particular, we have solved many problems that we have long wanted to solve but did not resolve. We have accomplished many important things that we wanted to do but did not do. In the new era, to better shoulder new tasks and embark on new journeys, and better cope with the "four tests" and "four dangers", our party must always be strong and build itself stronger.
　　To make the party stronger and stronger, we must first of all deeply grasp the new requirements of party building. We should thoroughly understand why the great struggle, the great project, the great cause and the great dream play a decisive role in the new great project of party building. Deeply comprehend the basic strategy of "article 14 adhere to the" especially "adhering to the party's leadership for all work" "adhere to the comprehensive governing party" the significance of the two, to grasp the party and government military and civilian, in the north and south, east and west, all the leadership of the party is "a major political principle connotation of The Times and" to self revolution, strictly party party "the party's most distinctive character; Grasp the rich connotation of constructing general requirements of new era, held on to strengthen the party's long-term ruling ability construction, the advanced nature and purity of the main line, catch this over the party's political construction, seize the firm ideal faith purpose of the foundation, seize the focus mobilize enthusiasm, initiative and creativity of the whole party. The more thoroughly and thoroughly understood the new requirements, the more firmly and consciously we can implement them, making our party always a pioneer of The Times and the backbone of the nation.
　　To make the party stronger and stronger, we must earnestly implement the eight important tasks. The 19th CPC national congress has made specific arrangements and put forward clear requirements. We should comprehensively implement them in light of actual conditions, and pay particular attention to political and ideological development. Highlighting the important position of political construction in party building is an important content of xi jinping thought on socialism with Chinese characteristics for a new era. The 19th CPC report put the political construction of the party in the first place and deployed it as the first important task, because the political construction of the party is the fundamental construction of the party and determines the direction and effect of the party's construction. Ideological construction is the foundation of the party. Xi jinping thought on socialism with Chinese characteristics for a new era is the soul of the 19th CPC report, the latest achievement in adapting marxism to Chinese conditions, and the guiding ideology the party must adhere to for a long time. Only armed with the party's innovation theory can we push the whole party to strive more consciously for the realization of the party's historical mission in the new era.
　　"The world will prosper, and its accumulation must have a source." A great cause must be led by a strong party. If we earnestly study and implement the 19th party spirit, build ourselves up and strengthen ourselves, and ensure that the party always wants to be with and work with the people, we will be able to steer the ship carrying the great dream of the Chinese people forward through the waves and sail triumphantly to the glorious shore.
